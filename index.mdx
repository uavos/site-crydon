# Secure Autonomy. Tactical Advantage.

**Crydon Defense Systems Inc.** is a U.S.-owned small business and nontraditional defense contractor focused on **attritable, scalable unmanned systems** designed for rapid fielding and iterative improvement through operator feedback.

**We build autonomy that survives real missions** — resilient avionics, mission-ready integration, and rapid prototyping for defense and aerospace programs.

---

## What We Do

- **Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)** R&D and prototyping  
- **Autonomous flight technologies** and advanced flight control integration  
- **LOS / BLOS data links & comms** for operational missions  
- **Systems engineering & payload integration** for defense/aerospace  
- Mission applications: **ISR**, disaster response, border security, maritime monitoring

---

## Why Crydon

**Founder-led engineering** with deep experience in UAV R&D and defense innovation.  
An **agile small-business structure** to move fast, integrate quickly, and deliver cost-effective results.

- Long-endurance and high-altitude UAV expertise
- Resilient avionics and mission-oriented integration
- Rapid prototyping and iterative field feedback loops
- Strong focus on **ITAR compliance**, cybersecurity, and sensitive data handling
