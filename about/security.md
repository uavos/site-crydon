# Security & Compliance

Crydon is committed to compliant and responsible engineering for defense and aerospace missions.

## ITAR & Export Compliance
We support program needs with careful handling of controlled technical data and export-sensitive information.

## Cybersecurity Practices
We apply cybersecurity hygiene appropriate for sensitive engineering work, including access control, least-privilege workflows, and secure collaboration practices.

## Sensitive Data Handling
We minimize exposure of mission data, control distribution, and maintain clear data-handling processes aligned with customer requirements.
