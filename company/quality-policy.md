# Quality Policy

Crydon Defense Systems Inc. is committed to delivering reliable engineering outcomes for mission-oriented programs.

## Our Quality Commitments

- Requirements traceability: define what “done” means early
- Verification-first mindset: plan tests as we design
- Configuration discipline: document changes and baselines
- Integration readiness: reduce surprises at test events
- Continuous improvement: iterate based on test evidence and user feedback

## Risk Management

We identify technical and schedule risks early and propose mitigation options with transparent tradeoffs.
