# Capabilities Overview

Crydon Defense Systems Inc. delivers engineering and prototyping for **unmanned aerial systems and autonomous flight technologies** with an emphasis on resilient integration and mission-ready results.

## Core Competencies

- Research, design, and development of **UAS and autonomy**
- **Advanced flight control systems** and autopilot software integration
- **LOS/BLOS communications** and data link architectures
- Systems engineering, rapid prototyping, and **payload integration**
- Mission applications:
  - ISR (intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance)
  - Disaster response
  - Border security
  - Maritime monitoring

## Differentiators

- **Founder-led** team with 15+ years of UAV R&D and defense innovation
- Expertise in **long-endurance, high-altitude** UAV programs and resilient avionics
- Agile small business structure enabling **rapid prototyping** and cost-effective delivery
- Prior success with SBIR-class efforts, DoD collaborations, and international test campaigns
- Strong commitment to **ITAR compliance**, cybersecurity practices, and sensitive data handling
